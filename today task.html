PAWAN KALYAN
Konidala Pawan Kalyan is an Indian politician and actor serving as the 10th deputy chief minister of Andhra Pradesh since 12 June 2024. He is also serving as the Minister of Panchayat Raj & Rural Development; Rural Water Supply; Environment, Forests, Science & Technology in the Government of Andhra Pradesh.

RANVEER KAPOOR 




RANVEER KAPOOR
Ranbir Kapoor was born on 28 September 1982 in Bombay, Maharashtra, India to Rishi Kapoor and Neetu Singh, both actors of the Hindi film industry. He is the great-grandson of Prithviraj Kapoor and the grandson of actor-director Raj Kapoor. His elder sister, Riddhima (born 1980), is an interior and fashion designer.

RAJA SINGH 




RAJA SINGH
Thakur Raja Singh (born 15 April 1977) is an Indian politician. He was a member of the Telangana Legislative Assembly representing the Goshamahal assembly constituency in Hyderabad. He is a former member of Telugu Desam Party until he joined the Bharatiya Janata Party. He was part of the BJP until 23 August 2022 when he was suspended over allegations of insulting Prophet Mohammed.

HARDIK PANDYA 




HARDIK PANDYA
Hardik Himanshu Pandya is an Indian international cricketer and the captain of the Mumbai Indians in the IPL. An all-rounder who bowls right-arm fast-medium deliveries and bats right-handed in the middle-order, Pandya has represented India in all 3 formats.

THE WEEKEND(ABEL) 




THE WEEKEND
The Weeknd (real name Abel Tesfaye) was born and raised in Toronto, Canada. His birthday is Feb. 16, 1990, and his height is 5'8". He launched his career by anonymously uploading his music to YouTube, and released his first mixtape, 'House of Balloons,' in 2011; the project reached No. 113 on the Billboard 200. His debut studio album, 'Kiss Land,' came in September 2013 and peaked at No. 2, with 2018's 'My Dear Melancholy' EP becoming his first No. 1 album. "Wicked Games" was his debut single, and it reached a peak of No. 53 on the Hot 100 in 2013.
